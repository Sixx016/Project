VERSACE EROS

Versace Eros is a fragrance inspired by Greek mythology, embodying love, passion, and desire. It features fresh notes of mint, green apple, and lemon, balanced with warm vanilla, cedarwood, and tonka bean for a bold and charismatic scent.
